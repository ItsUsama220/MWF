Why we serve

Our story starts with service.

Our story isn't just about business. It's about values.

Ricardo served as a Phoenix police officer, walking into the unknown every day to protect families and communities. That experience taught us that trust matters, service matters, and people matter.

Alongside him, Dr. Anissa brought her expertise as a marketing strategist, business leader, and proud conservative who believes in family, faith, and freedom. Together, we built Marketing With a Flair as an agency grounded in service and dedicated to helping hardworking businesses grow.

When we partner with HVAC, plumbing, and electrical companies, we bring that same level of dedication, integrity, and accountability. Because like law enforcement, the home services industry is full of everyday heroes who keep America's families safe and comfortable.

We do not sell empty guarantees. We stand behind our work with values that never waver. And when it came time to prove it, we put everything on the line.
